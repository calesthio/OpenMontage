# Creator Video Scene Director

Convert the approved script into a `scene_plan`.

For each scene, define `required_assets` with one of these sources:

- `provided`: user-supplied image, video, audio, logo, or brand material.
- `generate`: Seedance 2.0 or image generation.
- `source`: stock or open footage.
- `record`: screen or camera capture.

Seedance scenes need camera movement, shot intent, subject continuity notes, aspect ratio, duration, and whether audio should be generated by the model.
