# Arqentia — "The Unanswered Star" — keyword: star

**Format:** 9:16 IG+FB Reel ~38s. Remotion atelier + REAL Mixkit b-roll (avatar ≤20%: hook + CTA).
**ICP sector rotation:** LOCAL SERVICE businesses broadly — cafes, salons, gyms, clinics, shops (owner, 2nd person).
Grounding: BrightLocal Local Consumer Review Survey 2026 — **80%** of consumers are likely to use a
business that **responds to all of its reviews**; **42%** are unlikely to use one that never replies
(brightlocal.com/research/local-consumer-review-survey). A review is a public conversation the next
customer reads.

You got a five-star review. Nobody replied.

That silence costs you. Eighty percent choose businesses that reply. Forty-two percent avoid the ones that don't. Because a review isn't the finish line. It's a public conversation, and your next customer is reading it.

We call it the unanswered star.

The fix isn't hiring a social media manager. Every review gets a reply in two minutes, in your voice. Good ones get thanked. Bad ones get handled before they spread.

Your reviews are selling for you. Or against you.

Comment the word star. I'll send the review reply playbook.
